About

Hi, I'm Martin.

Self-directed learner exploring AI, LLMs, Linux, and hardware — mostly by building things and writing down what happened.

How I learn

I don't learn well from tutorials followed start to finish. I learn by picking a real question, building something to answer it, and hitting the parts the tutorial never covers.

That's the whole method behind Bronks Labs: building over watching, real hardware over toy demos, and writing it all down — including the parts that didn't work.

What I'm doing now

Running a local AI homelab on repurposed hardware, learning Linux and Docker through a headless home server, and using AI as a writing collaborator on a novel — all documented as it happens.

Current focus: expanding the headless server, building this site, and documenting the AI workflows as I go.
